Here is a timeline of the events we've done

Job Fair: 3/22/17

Our first high-school job fair took place on March 22, 2017 from 4:00-6:00 PM at Homestead High Schools’ cafeteria and hosted businesses like DACA, Wag Hotels, and Great America’s Kaman’s Art. We were incredibly successful, experiencing a total attendance of over 300 students, parents, and other members of the school district.

Resume Workshop: 4/19/17

We hosted a workshop at Lynbrook High School called Resumes that Impress. Mr. Tandun presented about how to improve and start a resume. The workshop was held at the Lynbrook High School auditorium and it was in partnership with Lynbrook BUILD and the Lynbrook College and Career Center.

Resume Workshop: 10/14/17

BAYCO will hosted a resume workshop on October 14th from 3:00-4:00 PM in Portable 2 at Fremont High School. It was a great opportunity for high schoolers to obtain insider tips on creating a professional resume and have professionals from LinkedIn and Fenwick & West review your resume

Interview Workshop: 10/27/17

BAYCO hosted a mock interview workshop on October 27th from 4:00-6:00 PM in Homestead High School's cafeteria. About 20 employees from Apple performed mock college and job interviews with groups of students.

Resume Workshop: 2/24/18

A Resume Reviewing Workshop took place at Cupertino Excel on Saturday, February 24, 2018 from 2:00-4:00 PM. The location was provided to us by the Student Internship Network (https://www.thesin.org/), a sub-organization of Excel Test Prep. This workshop gave the opportunity for students to listen to professionals on how to start their resumes and provided feedback on their resumes!

Job Fair March 21, 2018

Bayco held its annual Job Fair this year on March 21 at Monta Vista High School from 3:30 - 5:30 PM. The turnout was amazing with over 600 people attending and finding out about valauble internships, jobs, or summer programs. We had a lot of interest from companies such as Steve and Kates Camp, the City of Sunnyvale & Cupertino, and Apple. In total we had around 30 companies attend the fair! We look forward to making next year's job fair even better.
