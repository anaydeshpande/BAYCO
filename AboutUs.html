About Us

We are a group of high school students dedicated to helping others find internships

We are a part of the Fremont Union High Schools (FUHS) Foundation.

Some of you might even remember our first high-school job fair, which took place on March 22, 2017 and hosted businesses like DACA, Wag Hotels, and Great America’s Kaman’s Art. We were incredibly successful, experiencing a total attendance of over 300 students, parents, and other members of the community. Over the next school year, our focus will be to plan and market monthly career development workshops and an annual job fair for FUHSD students.
